Lambdoma Matrix Harmanically consonant Keyboard

Barbara Hero's matrix of wholenumber ratios organises the harmonic series into a unique keyboard. This online version aims to reproduce her innovative work and make it accessible to anyone. Best enjoyed on larger screens.
